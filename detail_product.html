Clair Q&A

How is Clair different from other AI-powered solutions available today?

Unlike generic AI solutions, Clair is designed specifically for healthcare. Our models are trained on our database of 200M+ clinical studies, 147k+ FDA inserts, 2M+ drug interactions and 100+ clinical guidelines, ensuring highly accurate and reliable medical information.  Our highly accurate answers also always come with references so results can be seen with context.

Why is Clair better than legacy medical reference tools?

Clair’s responses are ultra-fast, concise and specific to the query. Because our answers summarize data from multiple trusted sources, clinical teams won’t waste time searching through vague documentation. Legacy options also have a lag in their data feeds, but the Clair dataset is constantly refreshing real-time so there is no lag as new information is available.

How does Clair work?

Clair leverages large-scale language models trained on diverse medical texts to provide accurate, context-aware responses. It continuously learns from user interactions and feedback to refine its models, ensuring relevance and precision across various medical specialties. Our techniques include RAG, embeddings, complex pipelines and self learning.

What kinds of references drive Clair’s answers?

At a baseline, Clair references 200M+ Clinical Studies, 2M+ Drug Interactions, 147K+ FDA Package Inserts, and 100+ Clinical Guidelines. Our model uses intelligent sourcing, which means Clair selects the right source of information based on the use-case and the nature of the question. For example, asking Clair about the side effects of a drug will prompt the reference of FDA drug package inserts, then asking about the comparative efficacy of a drug to another will trigger Clair to use the NIH clinical trials database.

How does Clair ensure the accuracy and reliability of this data?

Clair integrates data in realtime, ensuring its database reflects the most current standards of care. Additionally, a team of Clair clinical experts reviews all data sources in a thorough vetting process, and continuously monitors search results to ensure all responses are accurate and credible. Our community of users can also rate and provide feedback on results, giving us the opportunity to continuously refine our results to be as accurate and helpful as possible.

Can I customize the sources for references or answer formatting?

Absolutely. Our Enterprise offering pairs you with Clair clinical and technology experts who help define custom modeling needs and streamline integrations with organizations’ unique or proprietary data.
