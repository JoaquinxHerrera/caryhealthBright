Modern Pharmacy Experience for Patients

End-to-end Process built to accommodate Rx processing & PDT activation

CaryConnect streamlines the process of accessing Digital Therapeutics through a seamless and efficient workflow.
Our dedicated team ensures swift access to prescribed digital therapies, delivering a frictionless patient journey and better health outcomes. Our platform is fully customizable to deliver your messaging and branding.
1.

PDT is prescribed to CaryRx and processed

Prescriptions are sent directly to CaryRx by healthcare providers. The CaryRx pharmacy team processes the prescription in minutes.
2.

CaryConnect sends patient SMS to white labeled payment page

Patients receive a text message notifying them that a new prescription has been received with a custom link to get started.
3.

Patient completes payment with flexible options.

CaryConnect offers a wide range of flexible payment options, including credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay and Klarna, ensuring convenience and accessibility for every patient.
4.

PDT Activated

After payment completion, your patient will immediately receive a link to activate their PDT and begin treatment.
