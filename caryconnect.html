CaryConnect Q&A

How does CaryConnect ensure data privacy and security?

CaryConnect is built with privacy and security in mind ensuring that data handling meets HIPAA and SOC 2 compliance standards.

Does CaryConnect handle prior authorizations or offer benefits investigation services?

Improve patient outcomes by using our benefits investigation program to reduce the time it takes to fill prescriptions. CaryConnect integrates real-time updates from our benefits investigation team using a proprietary CRM, ensuring efficient communication and oversight.

Is training or support provided for new users?

Yes, we offer onboarding, training, documentation and ongoing support to ensure users can maximize the value of CaryConnect.

Do you have experience working with limited distribution drugs or exclusive pharmacy networks?

Our team is well-versed in managing the unique requirements of these programs. We work closely with manufacturers to ensure compliance with all contractual and regulatory obligations, providing a seamless experience for prescribers and patients while maintaining the highest standards of accuracy and care.
