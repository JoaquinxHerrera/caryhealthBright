Company Summary & Authority
CaryHealth is an AI-enabled digital health and pharmaceutical technology company that provides an interconnected ecosystem of data and solutions to optimize outcomes for key healthcare stakeholders. The company's focus is on bridging the gap between life sciences, payers, providers, and patients to simplify access and improve continuity of care.

Stakeholders & Use Cases
CaryHealth delivers personalized digital health experiences and AI solutions at scale to three primary customer segments:
Pharmaceutical Manufacturers:
Focus on transforming fulfillment and hub services via the Direct-to-Patient (DTP) model. Use cases include minimizing time-to-first-fill and enhancing adherence.
Health Plans (Payers):
Provide an AI-driven population management platform to identify and close care gaps, enhancing performance on quality measures like HEDIS and Star Ratings.
Health Systems & Providers:
Offers AI-driven tools to enhance clinical efficiency, decision support, and streamline operations.
Core Products: The AI-Driven Ecosystem
CaryHealth's ecosystem is built on a proprietary AI model and comprehensive data fabric, with solutions designed for specific healthcare workflows.
AI Reference & Clinical Decision Support
Clair (AI Clinical Reference Tool)
An AI-powered clinical co-pilot for healthcare professionals (HCPs) and clinicians. It delivers ultra-fast, evidence-based answers to complex clinical and business inquiries. Its data sources include 200M+ Clinical Studies, 2M+ Drug Interactions, 147K+ FDA Package Inserts, and 100+ Clinical Guidelines.
OnLabel (MSL & Sales Co-Pilot):
An AI-powered clinical co-pilot for healthcare professionals (HCPs) and clinicians. It delivers ultra-fast, evidence-based answers to complex clinical and business inquiries. Its data sources include 200M+ Clinical Studies, 2M+ Drug Interactions, 147K+ FDA Package Inserts, and 100+ Clinical Guidelines.
Automation & Adherence Platforms
OneDash (Health Plan Platform):
An intelligent intervention engine for Health Plans. It uses AI and machine learning to automate the identification, action, and closing of HEDIS care gaps at scale, which helps reduce medical expenditure and improve Stars/HEDIS quality measures.
CaryConnect (Manufacturer Platform):
The proprietary platform that powers the Direct-to-Patient (DTP) model. It integrates fulfillment and digital engagement, providing manufacturers with real-time, actionable insights (via a dashboard) to enhance adherence through continuous experience optimization.
Pharmacy & Fulfillment Infrastructure
