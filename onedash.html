Closing Care Gaps with Clinical Sequencing Funnels

OneDash simplifies the management of complex healthcare workflows by structuring the process into a clear, step-by-step funnel. This organized, sequential approach enables targeted actions at each stage to address patient interventions more efficiently and effectively.

01.
Identify Patients in Need

Automatically searches for members who require intervention based on customizable criteria (e.g., patients lacking a specific prescription).

02.
Escalate Unresolved Steps

 If previous actions are unsuccessful, the system escalates interventions, ensuring continuous follow-up.

03.
Layered Action Strategy

 Each layer represents a step in the intervention sequence, allowing you to focus on patients needing the most attention.

04.
Automated Gap Closure

With automation built in, OneDash reduces manual work and helps you close gaps faster, ultimately driving better outcomes.

OneDash Q&A

What is OneDash, and how can it benefit my organization?

OneDash is a healthcare data intelligence platform that enables organizations to monitor, manage, and act on patient interventions efficiently. By centralizing and automating intervention lifecycles, it helps healthcare providers improve outcomes, streamline workflows, and support clinical and operational decision-making.

How quickly can OneDash be deployed in our system?

OneDash is designed to be ready for deployment quickly. In many cases, it can be online and operational within a few weeks, allowing your organization to start benefiting from its features without long wait times.

Can OneDash be customized for our specific needs?

Yes, OneDash offers customizable features, including intervention programs tailored to your organization’s goals, as well as custom roles, privileges, and views that support unique workflows within your organization.

What types of data sources can OneDash integrate with?

OneDash supports integration with a wide variety of data sources, both internal and external. It can ingest, blend, and report on disparate data sources from within your organization and third-party providers, giving you a comprehensive view of your data.

How does OneDash ensure data security and privacy?

OneDash complies with healthcare data security standards to ensure your data remains secure. With secure data integration and comprehensive reporting, it aligns with industry regulations and best practices for healthcare data management.

How does OneDash support clinical measure reporting?

OneDash continuously updates clinical measures, including Star Ratings, HEDIS, and other key performance indicators. It recalculates these metrics daily or hourly, ensuring that your organization always has access to the most current data.
