OnLabel Q&A

What does the implementation process look like?

Our streamlined process includes selecting your use case, connecting data and documents, customizing settings, thorough testing, launching OnLabel, and receiving ongoing support to ensure success.

How does OnLabel maintain data security?

Our team prioritizes data security with custom data handled on a separate, HIPAA-compliant server, ensuring the highest standards of data security and privacy. Our SOC 2 compliance further guarantees that your data is managed with integrity and care.

Can OnLabel be customized to my organization?

Our team will work with you to identify and tailor OnLabel to your use case including whitelabeled UI, specific compliance needs, or unique data types.

Does OnLabel integrate with my team’s existing CRM or other software?

Absolutely, we currently have existing integrations with OnLabel including Salesforce. Reach out to our team to learn more about how we integrate.

What is Clair & how is it related to OnLabel?

Clair is a breakthrough clinical AI reference, on a mission to make AI-driven efficiencies readily available and adaptable to all spaces in healthcare, through a trusted and easy-to-use platform. Clair acts as the foundation for OnLabel’s search and inquiry features. Learn more about Clair.

What kinds of references drive Clair’s answers?

At a baseline, Clair references 200M+ Clinical Studies, 2M+ Drug Interactions, 147K+ FDA Package Inserts, and 100+ Clinical Guidelines. Our model uses intelligent sourcing, which means Clair selects the right source of information based on the use-case and the nature of the question. For example, asking Clair about the side effects of a drug will prompt the reference of FDA drug package inserts, then asking about the comparative efficacy of a drug to another will trigger Clair to use the NIH clinical trials database.
