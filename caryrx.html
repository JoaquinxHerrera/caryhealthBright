CaryRx Q&A

Is CaryRx a pharmacy?

Yes, we distribute directly from our pharmacy located in Washington, DC.
‍
CaryRx’s founders have backgrounds in both pharmacy and technology. We built CaryRx because you deserve a pharmacy that can provide an amazing mobile experience.

You will love your new pharmacy.

What type of prescriptions do you accept?

Have your provider send prescriptions directly to CaryRx by phone, fax or electronic prescribing. Just let them know the following:

E-Prescribe: CaryRx
Phone: 202-930-4242
Fax: 202-930-4243
NCPDP: 0905250
Address: 1300 7th St NW, Suite 200, Washington DC 20001

You can also transfer prescriptions in just a few taps from other pharmacies in our mobile app. If you need assistance, you can get in touch with us easily via chat.

Do you take my insurance?

CaryRx accepts the majority of plans, including medicare.

Who are CaryRx’s pharmacists?

All of CaryRx’s pharmacists are licensed pharmacists with doctorates in pharmacy. They are all extremely knowledgeable, so send them a chat and ask them questions about your medications or health!
